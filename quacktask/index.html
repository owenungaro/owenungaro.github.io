QuackTask is a Chrome extension that helps Stevens Institute of Technology students stay on top of their coursework.

What it does:

  • Uses OAuth to connect to your Google account
  • Scrapes your assignments from Canvas (using Better Canvas)
  • Pushes your due dates to Google Tasks automatically
  • Lets you choose which task list to use

Why it needs permissions:
